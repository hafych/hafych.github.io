*   **Children’s Privacy & P2P Data Disclosure:** We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. However, due to the Peer-to-Peer (P2P) nature of certain Apps in our portfolio (which may utilize Tor and WebRTC), technical network data (such as your IP address, WebRTC connection candidates, and network ports) must be transmitted directly to other users or network relays you interact with.
